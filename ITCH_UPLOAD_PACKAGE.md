## Full Description (itch.io page body)

```
PHENIX: NULL EDEN is an HTML5 Canvas 2D cyber-survival roguelite, playable free in your browser.

Choose your survivor from a growing roster of playable characters — each with unique weapons,
abilities, and playstyles. Fight through escalating Endless waves of cyber enemies, elite units,
and major bosses. Collect relics to build your passive loadout and watch your active relic HUD
fill with glowing upgrades.

At the 5-minute mark in Endless mode, the NULL BREACH ARENA triggers — a high-pressure boss
gauntlet with cycling mini-bosses, major bosses, and attack aircraft. Survive it to earn arena
fragments and unlock arena-exclusive relics.

Between runs, EDEN CORE transmits. The Boss Echo Archive records every major boss you kill.
Eden Memory tracks your milestones. System Logs deliver EDEN CORE's narrative transmissions
as you push deeper. Chaos Laws — a new selection system — will reshape your next run.

---

Key Features:

• Multiple playable survivors with distinct combat styles
• Fast-paced cyber-survival combat with enemies, elites, and bosses
• Relic system: universal, boss, character, and arena-exclusive relics
• Active relic HUD — see your equipped passive stack at a glance
• EDEN CORE narrative system: transmissions, logs, and archive
• NULL BREACH ARENA: endgame boss gauntlet at 5:00 and 12:00
• Boss Echo Archive: your kill history with passive bonuses
• Eden Memory: milestone progression timeline
• System Logs: lore and EDEN CORE transmissions
• Chaos Laws Preview: emergent run-modifier system (Selection V1 active)
• Mobile touch controls (virtual joystick + action buttons)
• Keyboard support
• Controller support (Gamepad API — see notes below)

---

Controller Note:
Controller support is implemented and PS5-style mappings are supported by the browser Gamepad
API, but some devices and browsers may require a first button press or OS pairing before the
browser detects the controller. Not all browser/controller combinations are guaranteed.

---

Development Status:
Playable public demo — work in progress. Balance, content, and polish are actively evolving.
Feedback is very welcome.
```

## Controls (copy to itch.io controls field)

```
Keyboard:
  WASD / Arrow Keys — move
  Shift — dash
  Q — Pulse Shield
  E — EMP Cloud
  Space — Ultimate
  ESC — pause / menu

Controller (Gamepad API):
  Left Stick / D-Pad — move / navigate menus
  Cross / A — confirm
  Circle / B — back
  L1 / LB — Q (Pulse Shield)
  R1 / RB — E (EMP Cloud)
  Triangle / Y — Ultimate
  L2 / LT — dash
  R2 / RT — dash

Mobile:
  Virtual joystick (bottom-left)
  On-screen Q / E / Shift / Ultimate buttons
  Landscape orientation recommended
```
