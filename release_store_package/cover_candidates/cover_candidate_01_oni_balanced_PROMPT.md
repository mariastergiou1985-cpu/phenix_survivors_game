## WHY THIS CANDIDATE

The balanced hero-vs-boss composition is the most commercially proven formula for action roguelite covers. The viewer sees two things instantly: someone powerful (Oni) and something that dwarfs them (MATRIX ANNIHILATOR). That tension creates a click. Used by Hades, Dead Cells, Risk of Rain 2, Returnal.

This is the version to generate first and to use as the primary itch.io cover.

**Screenshots that inspired this candidate:**
- `screenshot_06_boss_MATRIX_ANNIHILATOR_INBOUND.png` — boss scale, crimson projectile storm, dramatic lighting
- `screenshot_01_main_menu.png` — void-black atmosphere, cyan grid, gold PHENIX title placement
- `screenshot_08_chaos_combat.png` — energy density, dark field intensity

## MAIN PROMPT

```
Premium dark cyberpunk indie game cover key art. Game: "PHENIX: NULL EDEN", a cyber-survival roguelite.

HERO — ONI WARRIOR (lower center, 35% of frame height): A powerful Oni warrior in heavy dark cyberpunk plate armor with demonic Oni mask/visor — glowing cyan eye slits, dark cracked armor with neon cyan rune markings along joints and gauntlets. Mid-Cataclysm Shockwave attack: both armored fists thrust forward or down, erupting with a burst of cyan and metallic gold energy that pulses outward across the ground. Full body clearly visible. Facing slightly left, turned toward viewer. Stance: powerful, planted, not fleeing — this is a fighter, not a victim. Strong rim lighting in neon cyan from below and metallic gold from the energy burst. Dark armor absorbs the background while the energy makes the silhouette pop.

BOSS — MATRIX ANNIHILATOR (upper center, 45% of frame height): Looms above and behind the hero. Massive angular geometric crystalline machine — black lattice body, hard edges, no organic curves. Think corrupted digital infrastructure given physical form. Scale: it should feel the size of a building. Glowing crimson-red eyes (two large eye-points, blazing forward toward the viewer). Null-red electric arcs and digital discharge sweep outward from its mass in jagged curves across the upper half of the frame. Its presence creates the ceiling of the image — the hero is literally standing under its shadow. It should look like it cannot be fought and yet is being fought.

COMPOSITION: Hero anchors the lower center. Boss anchors the upper center. Between them: the tension. There is open negative space between the hero's charge and the boss's form — this gap is where the danger lives. The two elements face each other across it. Visual balance: the boss is larger but the hero is brighter — their energy is the color contrast.

BACKGROUND: Pure deep void (#04060C near-black with very slight blue tint). Faint neon cyan hexagonal or square grid lines recede toward the center horizon — barely 15% opacity, atmospheric depth only. The grid suggests a digital environment without competing with the art. Upper background: the boss's null-red aura creates a dark red ambient glow that grades into the black. Lower background: the hero's cyan energy creates a pool of light on the ground plane.

FOREGROUND EDGES: Lower left corner: silhouette of Walker (tall cloaked figure, in shadow). Lower right corner: 2 enemy silhouettes (humanoid, in motion). All edge silhouettes are dark — 10–20% visible, serving as depth layers not characters.

PARTICLES: Cyan data shards spiral outward from the hero's fists during the shockwave. Floating gold XP-orb shapes in the air between hero and boss. Red static discharge fragments fall from the boss perimeter toward the ground. These are the particle language of the game — sparse enough to feel intentional, not a chaos cloud.

TITLE TYPOGRAPHY — EMBEDDED IN IMAGE: In the top third of the frame, between the boss form and the top edge. "PHENIX: NULL EDEN" — large, angular geometric cyberpunk display font (similar to Orbitron or Exo 2 Bold Condensed), metallic gold fill with a layered neon cyan outer glow that bleeds 3–4px outward. The title text should feel lit — as if the neon is emanating from the letters. Below the title, approximately one-third the font size: "A CYBER-SURVIVAL ROGUELITE" in small caps, dim muted cyan (#2EE6F6 at 60% opacity), clean, no glow. Both lines left-aligned or centered — centered preferred. Title must be fully legible when the image is scaled to 300px wide. No other text anywhere in the image.

COLOR RULES — STRICT: Background #04060C. Oni armor: dark charcoal + black. Oni energy: #00f5ff cyan + #FFD700 gold. Boss body: near-black with dark geometry. Boss glow: #ff003c null-red. Boss arcs: #ff003c → dark red gradient. Title: #FFD700 gold + #2EE6F6 cyan glow. Particles: mix of all three (cyan, gold, red). No neon pink, no magenta, no lime green, no pastel blue, no off-palette contamination.

LIGHTING SUMMARY: Top half = crimson-dark (boss aura). Bottom half = cyan-gold (hero energy). Deep black center where they meet. High contrast — no mid-tone grey washes.

STYLE: High-fidelity digital concept art / game key art. Rendered as a premium illustration — NOT pixel art, NOT anime, NOT chibi, NOT mobile cartoon, NOT photorealistic people. Closest visual register: Hades (Supergiant), Dead Cells (Motion Twin), Returnal (Housemarque). Dark, adult, premium, intentional.

MOOD: The grid is collapsing. The machine is everything. The Oni is the only one left standing.

DO NOT INCLUDE: HUD elements, health bars, XP bars, kill counters, any in-game UI overlay, Phasewalker character (white/silver female ninja with katana), Chaos Law selection panels, COMING SOON labels, award badges, review scores, platform logos (Steam/PlayStation/Xbox), screenshot-collage montage, multiple duplicated heroes, mobile controls/virtual joystick, debug text of any kind, soft pastel tones, oversaturated neon bloom that washes the image into a light fog, distorted hands or weapons, unreadable title text, AI face artifacts (generate with mask or obscure the Oni face behind the mask/visor).
```

## COMMERCIAL STRENGTH: 9/10

**Strengths:** Proven formula. Maximum information density at small size. Strong visual tension. Oni mask is an iconic readable silhouette. MATRIX ANNIHILATOR scale creates immediate threat perception. Title placement above boss is clean and clear.

**Risks:**
- Oni character may need very clear mask/visor design — avoid generic armored human face without distinctive feature
- Boss geometry must not read as abstract background texture — ensure it has physical presence (use depth, rim lighting, scale cues)
- If particles are too dense, title may be visually competing — prompt specifies sparse particles; iterate if this occurs
- The empty center space between hero and boss must not feel like dead space — particles bridge it
