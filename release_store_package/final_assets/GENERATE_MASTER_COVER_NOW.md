Its presence creates the ceiling of the image — the hero is literally standing under its shadow. It should look like it cannot be fought and yet is being fought.

COMPOSITION: Hero anchors the lower center. Boss anchors the upper center. Between them: the tension. There is open negative space between the hero's charge and the boss's form — this gap is where the danger lives. The two elements face each other across it. Visual balance: the boss is larger but the hero is brighter — their energy is the color contrast.

BACKGROUND: Pure deep void (#04060C near-black with very slight blue tint). Faint neon cyan hexagonal or square grid lines recede toward the center horizon — barely 15% opacity, atmospheric depth only. The grid suggests a digital environment without competing with the art. Upper background: the boss's null-red aura creates a dark red ambient glow that grades into the black. Lower background: the hero's cyan energy creates a pool of light on the ground plane.

FOREGROUND EDGES: Lower left corner: silhouette of Walker (tall cloaked figure, in shadow). Lower right corner: 2 enemy silhouettes (humanoid, in motion). All edge silhouettes are dark — 10–20% visible, serving as depth layers not characters.

PARTICLES: Cyan data shards spiral outward from the hero's fists during the shockwave. Floating gold XP-orb shapes in the air between hero and boss. TITLE TYPOGRAPHY — EMBEDDED IN IMAGE: In the top third of the frame, between the boss form and the top edge. "PHENIX: NULL EDEN" — large, angular geometric cyberpunk display font (similar to Orbitron or Exo 2 Bold Condensed), metallic gold fill with a layered neon cyan outer glow that bleeds 3–4px outward. The title text should feel lit — as if the neon is emanating from the letters. Below the title, approximately one-third the font size: "A CYBER-SURVIVAL ROGUELITE" in small caps, dim muted cyan (#2EE6F6 at 60% opacity), clean, no glow. Both lines left-aligned or centered — centered preferred. Title must be fully legible when the image is scaled to 300px wide. No other text anywhere in the image.

COLOR RULES — STRICT: Background #04060C. Oni armor: dark charcoal + black. Oni energy: #00f5ff cyan + #FFD700 gold. Boss body: near-black with dark geometry. Boss glow: #ff003c null-red. Boss arcs: #ff003c → dark red gradient. Title: #FFD700 gold + #2EE6F6 cyan glow. Particles: mix of all three (cyan, gold, red). No neon pink, no magenta, no lime green, no pastel blue, no off-palette contamination.

LIGHTING SUMMARY: Top half = crimson-dark (boss aura). Bottom half = cyan-gold (hero energy). Deep black center where they meet. High contrast — no mid-tone grey washes.

STYLE: High-fidelity digital concept art / game key art. Rendered as a premium illustration — NOT pixel art, NOT anime, NOT chibi, NOT mobile cartoon, NOT photorealistic people. Closest visual register: Hades (Supergiant), Dead Cells (Motion Twin), Returnal (Housemarque). Dark, adult, premium, intentional.

MOOD: The grid is collapsing. The machine is everything. The Oni is the only one left standing.

DO NOT INCLUDE: HUD elements, health bars, XP bars, kill counters, any in-game UI overlay, Phasewalker character (white/silver female ninja with katana), Chaos Law selection panels, COMING SOON labels, award badges, review scores, platform logos (Steam/PlayStation/Xbox), screenshot-collage montage, multiple duplicated heroes, mobile controls/virtual joystick, debug text of any kind, soft pastel tones, oversaturated neon bloom that washes the image into a light fog, distorted hands or weapons, unreadable title text, AI face artifacts (generate with mask or obscure the Oni face behind the mask/visor).
