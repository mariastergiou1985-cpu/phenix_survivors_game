## 3. STRENGTHS AND WEAKNESSES

### Candidate 1 — Oni Balanced
**Strengths:** Highest commercial score. Lowest generation risk. Clean title placement. Oni mask is universally readable as "action hero." Balanced composition works at every crop size. Best itch.io fit.
**Weaknesses:** Demon warrior archetype appears on many cyberpunk games — less distinctive. Boss may feel smaller relative to V2 if generation doesn't nail the scale difference.

### Candidate 2 — Oni Boss-Dominant
**Strengths:** Most cinematic. Best Steam header capsule by composition. Boss eye focal point survives any thumbnail size. Communicates overwhelming threat better than any other candidate.
**Weaknesses:** Title placement is the hardest of the three — requires iteration if boss form and title compete. Hero can read as too small on itch.io 630×500 format. Not the strongest itch.io primary cover.

### Candidate 3 — Skeleton Warrior
**Strengths:** Highest unique identity score. Most memorable cover if it generates correctly. Best Steam library capsule (600×900) candidate. Sets the game apart from genre peers.
**Weaknesses:** Highest generation risk — AI consistently drifts to zombie horror for skeleton characters. Multiple iterations likely required. Narrower appeal than Oni in general store browsing. Minor risk of misleading genre expectation.

## 4. REQUIRED FIXES (PRE-GENERATION)

No fixes required to the prompts before generating. All three prompts are production-ready.

**One pre-generation recommendation:** For Candidate 3 specifically, open the prompt file and add this phrase at the very beginning of the main prompt (before the composition description):

```
CRITICAL STYLE NOTE: This is a CYBERPUNK ARMORED WARRIOR with a skeletal aesthetic — NOT a zombie, NOT a horror creature, NOT Halloween. The character should feel like a powerful video game hero who happens to have bone-plated cyberpunk armor. Do not generate horror, gore, decay, or undead monster imagery.
```

This reduces zombie-drift risk in tools like DALL-E that are sensitive to early framing.
