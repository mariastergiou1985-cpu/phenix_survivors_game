## ABOUT THIS GAME

The Grid is alive and it wants you dead.

PHENIX: NULL EDEN is a fast-paced cyber-survival roguelite set in the NULL EDEN network — a corrupted digital realm where entities multiply, bosses evolve, and Chaos Mode waits at the edge of every Endless run.

Pick a character. Build your protocol. Fight through escalating waves. Collect relics. Face bosses. And when the clock hits 21:00 in Endless — survive what comes next.

## LONG DESCRIPTION / STORE BODY

### Three Modes. One Escalating Threat.

**Act 1** — The entry point. Survive 21 minutes of escalating waves, collect upgrades and relics, and face the bosses the NULL EDEN network throws at you. A complete, satisfying run every time.

**Endless Mode** — No timer. No ceiling. Unlock Endless after your first Act 1 run and push as far as your build can carry you. Permanent records tracked across every session.

**Chaos Mode** — Activated at 21:00 Endless, or entered directly if unlocked. The music changes. The enemies change. The pressure compounds. Your Chaos Survival Rank — BRONZE, SILVER, GOLD, PLATINUM — is earned by how long you endure after the world breaks.

---

### Seven Characters. Seven Ultimate Protocols.

Each character in NULL EDEN has a distinct playstyle, a unique ultimate ability, and a different risk/reward balance. Mastery is not given — it is earned through runs.

- **Skeleton Warrior** — Bone Guard Blast. Durable frontliner.
- **Cyber Arm Hero** — Overdrive Beam. Precision at range.
- **Taekwondo Girl** — Lightning Dash Strike. Burst mobility.
- **Brawler Warrior** — Close-range aggression. No mercy.
- **Assassin Clone** — Plasma weapons. Speed and positioning.
- **Oni Cataclysm Protocol** — Cataclysm Shockwave. Maximum radius.
- **Euclid Vector** — Toxin Kit multi-spread. Target saturation.

### Bosses That Mean Something

NULL EDEN's 7 bosses and mini-bosses are not decoration. Each one arrives with distinct attack patterns, escalating damage, and a specific moment in your run where they test everything you've built. There is no skipping them.

### Build, Persist, Grow

- **Relics** — passive power compounds across your run. Stack wisely.
- **Upgrades** — a layered card selection system. Every run offers different paths.
- **Grid Credits** — permanent currency. Carry over between runs. Unlock characters, power-ups, permanent boosts.
- **Chaos Laws** — select a law before entering Chaos Mode. Each reshapes the rules of engagement. Additional laws in post-launch updates.
- **Achievements** — earned by Endless milestones and Chaos survivorship.

---

### Walker

A companion ally who joins your run at key moments — the Walker NPC provides support, dialogue, and presence during the hardest stretches of Endless and Chaos.

### Full Controller + Touch Support

PHENIX: NULL EDEN is built for multiple input methods. Not as an afterthought — as a design commitment.

- **Controller** — PS5 DualSense, Xbox, PC gamepad. Full support across all menus, character select, gameplay, pause, Chaos Law overlay, and results screen.
- **Mobile/Touch** — virtual joystick and action buttons. Full landscape play. Tested on physical devices.
- **Keyboard + Mouse** — standard full support.

### Original Music
