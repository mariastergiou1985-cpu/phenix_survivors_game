## itch.io PRICING MECHANICS

- itch.io supports "Pay what you want above minimum" OR fixed price
- For a paid premium game, use **fixed price** (no "pay what you want" — it signals a donation project, not a commercial game)
- Set price in EUR — itch.io converts automatically for other currencies
- Can enable a "Download for free" demo mode separately if desired (not recommended at launch — keep it paid)
- Optional: "No Thanks, I'll just browse" should be hidden (itch setting) for a paid game

---

## STEAM PRICING MECHANICS

- Steam pricing is set per-region in Steamworks
- Set EUR as the primary price — Valve converts to USD, GBP, etc.
- Launch discounts require at least 30 days between discounts (Steam policy)
- Do NOT set a launch discount higher than 40% on a new release without checking current Steam guidelines
