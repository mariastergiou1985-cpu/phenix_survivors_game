### ⚡ ENTER NULL EDEN

The Grid is collapsing. Corrupted entities flood every sector. A signal has activated — your Protocol is live.

PHENIX: NULL EDEN drops you into a neon cyber-survival arena where every second matters, every upgrade counts, and Chaos Mode is waiting at the edge of every Endless run.

---

### 🔫 SURVIVE THE GRID

Face relentless enemy waves across three escalating game modes:

- **Act 1** — A timed survival run. Reach 21 minutes. Prove you belong.
- **Endless Mode** — No timer. No ceiling. Survive as long as your build holds.
- **Chaos Mode** — Unleashed at 21:00 Endless. Music shifts. Rules break. Everything accelerates. Earn your rank: BRONZE · SILVER · GOLD · PLATINUM.

---

### 🧬 CHOOSE YOUR PROTOCOL

Seven playable characters. Each one plays differently. Each one demands a different strategy.

- **Skeleton Warrior** — Bone Guard Blast. Tanky and relentless.
- **Cyber Arm Hero** — Overdrive Beam. Precision power.
- **Taekwondo Girl** — Lightning Dash Strike. Speed and burst.
- **Brawler Warrior** — Raw aggression. Close-range dominance.
- **Assassin Clone** — Plasma weapons, speed, and positioning. High risk.
- **Oni Cataclysm Protocol** — Cataclysm Shockwave. Maximum destruction.
- **Euclid Vector** — Toxin Kit, multi-target specialist.

*Japan Phasewalker — COMING SOON.*

### 💀 BOSSES, RELICS, CHAOS

- **7 bosses and mini-bosses** — each with distinct patterns and escalating threat
- **Relics** — passive power items that compound across your run
- **Upgrades** — a layered selection system that shapes every build differently
- **Permanent progression** — Grid Credits carry over between runs. Unlock more. Grow stronger.
- **Chaos Laws** — choose your law before entering Chaos. Each law reshapes the rules. (Additional laws coming post-launch.)
- **Chaos Survival Rank** — your time inside Chaos is measured and ranked every run. Beat your best.
- **Walker** — a companion ally who appears during key moments. Don't leave without them.

## CONTROLS SECTION

**Keyboard + Mouse**
- WASD / Arrow keys — movement
- Mouse — aim
- Click — fire / interact
- Q / E — abilities
- Space — ultimate
- Shift — dash
- ESC — pause
- M — mute

**Controller (PS5 / Xbox / PC Gamepad)**
- Left stick — movement
- Right stick — aim
- Right trigger / A / X — fire
- Buttons — abilities, ultimate, dash
- Start / Options — pause
- Full menu navigation supported

**Mobile / Touch**
- Virtual joystick (left side) — movement
- Action buttons (right side) — attack, dash, Q, E, ultimate
- Tap — interact / menu
- Landscape orientation required
- Fullscreen button included
